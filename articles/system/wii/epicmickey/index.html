Epic Mickey

Info

Developed by: Junction Point Studios / Disney
Devlopment Started: 2004
Released: 2010

Epic Mickey is an action adventure/platformer released on the Nintendo Wii. The game is centered around Mickey visiting a world of forgotten Disney characters, the "Wasteland", after acidentally causing havok in their world. Mickey must defeat the Blot and escape Wasteland.
