Epic Mickey

Predevelopment

Epic Mickey's creation involved a lot of planning inside Disney, even before a devloper was chosen.

GI Demo

Before starting full development on the game, Junction Point created a demo for GameInformer to show off the concept.

Beta/Alpha

Secrecy has kept many of the early game hidden. Only a few things have surfaced from this era.

Release

Junction Point was careful to not leave early things in the game, though some interesting items were left behind.

Timeline

Epic Mickey experienced a very long development. Disney think tanks, leaked concept art, and a brand new studio spread across the game's six year long development cycle.
